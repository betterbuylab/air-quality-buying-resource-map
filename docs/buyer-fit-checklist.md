# Buyer-fit checklist

- [ ] Is the room problem dust and pollen, or persistent dampness?
- [ ] Do pets add dander load that CADR sizing must cover?
- [ ] Is the target room a bedroom, basement, or open living area?
- [ ] Can you route continuous drain for a dehumidifier?
- [ ] Is noise at night a constraint for bedroom placement?
- [ ] Are you treating symptoms while investigating moisture sources?
- [ ] Do filter replacement costs fit your long-term budget?
- [ ] Is one portable unit enough, or do multiple rooms need coverage?
