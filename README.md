---

## Buyer-fit questions

1. Is the room problem dust and pollen, or persistent dampness?
2. Do pets add dander load that CADR sizing must cover?
3. Is the target room a bedroom, basement, or open living area?
4. Can you route continuous drain for a dehumidifier?
5. Is noise at night a constraint for bedroom placement?
6. Are you treating symptoms while investigating moisture sources?
7. Do filter replacement costs fit your long-term budget?
8. Is one portable unit enough, or do multiple rooms need coverage?
